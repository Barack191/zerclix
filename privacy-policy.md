# VYBZ Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how VYBZ collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our app.

1. Information we collect
- Email address (when you sign up)
- Username and profile information (display name, avatar, bio)
- Content you create (posts, stories, comments)
- Messages you send (DMs and message requests)
- Usage data (analytics, device information)

2. How we use your information
- To provide and maintain the service
- To let you create and share content
- To send transactional messages (verification, password reset)
- To improve the service and investigate abuse

3. How you can delete your account
- You can delete your account from Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.
- Deleting your account will remove your user profile and local data stored in the app.
- Note: This demo app stores data locally (client-side). For production, deletion would remove server-side records.

4. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, contact: privacy@vybz.example
